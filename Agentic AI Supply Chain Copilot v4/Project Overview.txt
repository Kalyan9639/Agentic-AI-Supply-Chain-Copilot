*Detailed Project Description – Agentic AI Supply-Chain Copilot for Indian Agri & Retail*

### *1. What is this project in 1 line?*
An AI assistant that watches news, weather, and strikes 24/7 like a human manager, thinks about risks to your business, and automatically takes action to save money and prevent losses – while you sleep.

### *2. What problem does it solve?*
*Main problem for Indian businesses like Undua/farmers/traders:*  
“You only know about a truck strike, cyclone, or port closure _after_ your goods are already stuck. By then you lose lakhs in spoiled crops, penalties, or emergency transport.”

*This project solves:*  
1. *Late warnings* → Gives 2-3 weeks early alert instead of 2 hours  
2. *No action* → Doesn’t just warn. It books new truck, finds new supplier, updates your records automatically  
3. *Information overload* → Reads 1000 news sources, but tells you only 1 line: “Strike tomorrow. I booked Tata Ace. Loss avoided: ₹1.9L”

### *3. What exactly does the AI do? Simple example*
You’re a tomato trader in Hyderabad.

*Without this AI:*  
3 AM: Telangana lorry strike announced.  
8 AM: You wake up. 2 tons tomatoes at farm, no truck. They rot. Loss ₹2 lakh.

*With this AI:*  
3:05 AM: AI reads strike news → Checks your Google Sheet → Sees “Order #452: 2 tons tomato to Hyderabad” → Remembers “2023 strike delayed 3 days” → Decides → Books 5 mini-trucks from alternate vendor → WhatsApps farmer “Load by 6 AM” → Updates your Sheet.  
8 AM: You wake up to message “Strike handled. Extra cost ₹8k. Saved ₹1.92L.”

### *4. Two modes of operation*
Mode | When it runs | What happens | Who starts it
**Mode 1: Proactive Agent** | Every 15 min, 24/7 | AI scans news/weather → Thinks → Acts on its own → Sends you WhatsApp only if action taken | Automatic scheduler. No human needed
**Mode 2: Reactive Chatbot** | When you ask | You: “Will Vizag port close?” → AI searches memory → Answers with source | You type question
*For JNTUH project*: Mode 1 is the main innovation. Mode 2 is bonus feature.

### *5. How data flows – End to end in simple steps*
Step 1: WATCH → Every 15 minutes
Sources: News websites, Govt weather alerts, Social media posts, Port updates, Your Google Sheet orders
Tool used: Data scrapers / public APIs

Step 2: UNDERSTAND → Convert to AI format  
New info like "Cyclone near Vizag" is converted to numbers using a free embedding model
Tool used: Local embedding model that runs on your computer, no internet cost

Step 3: REMEMBER → Save + recall past cases
Stores new event in local memory database. Also searches: "Did Vizag close last year? What worked?"
Tool used: Free local vector database on your laptop

Step 4: THINK → Brain makes decision
Gemma model from Hugging Face gets: 
1. Your business profile: "I sell tomatoes, Hyderabad, low risk"
2. New event: "Cyclone Apr 25-27"
3. Active orders: From your Google Sheet
4. Past memory: "2023: Shifted to Chennai port, saved loss"
Gemma replies: "Action: Yes. Reroute Order #452 to Chennai. Reason: ..."

Step 5: ACT → AI uses tools
Calls real-world functions: Book truck, Send WhatsApp, Update Google Sheet
Tool used: Free APIs for WhatsApp, transport booking

Step 6: INFORM → You get summary
WhatsApp: "Cyclone alert. I rerouted your shipment. ETA +2 days. No action needed."
Google Sheet cell updated: "Agent handled - Rerouted"
### *6. What makes it "Agentic AI" not just a chatbot?*
Normal Chatbot | Your Agentic Copilot
Waits for you to ask | Runs on its own every 15 min
Only gives text answer | Books truck, sends WhatsApp, edits Sheet
No memory | Remembers last year’s monsoon solution
One step | Plans 5 steps: Check → Decide → Book → Inform → Log
*Agentic = Has agency to act*, like a real employee.

### *7. How does it know MY business?*
You teach it once on Day 1. Three ways:
1. *Business Profile File*: You write “I trade tomatoes in Hyderabad. Main suppliers: X, Y. Rule: Never buy above ₹30/kg.” AI reads this every time.
2. *Google Sheet Tabs*: One tab called `Business_Profile` + `Suppliers` + `Orders`. AI checks this before acting.
3. *Memory Database*: You add one line: “Ravi Agro Traders main risk is monsoon + Telangana strikes.” AI recalls this during reasoning.

So it won’t suggest buying cement if you sell vegetables.

### *8. Data Sources – Where does info come from?*
You’ll pull from these types of sources. All have free tiers:
Type of Data | Why needed | Example sources
**News & Geopolitics** | Wars, bans, strikes | Public news APIs, Govt press releases
**Weather Alerts** | Cyclone, flood, heatwave | National weather dept APIs, free weather APIs
